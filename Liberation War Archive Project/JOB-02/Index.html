Women of the Revolution

From the battlefields to secret intelligence networks, women played a pivotal role in the liberation of Bangladesh.

  • Combatants: Figures like Taramon Bibi and Sitara Begum who fought on the front lines.
  • Shadhin Bangla Betar Kendra: Women who kept the nation's spirit alive through radio and music.
  • Grassroots Support: Thousands who provided food, shelter, and medical aid to the Mukti Bahini.

"The sacrifice of our mothers and sisters is the foundation upon which this nation stands. Their courage remains a cornerstone of our archive."
