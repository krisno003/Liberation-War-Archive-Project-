Shadhin Bangla Betar Kendra

Operating from secret locations, this radio station was the heartbeat of the revolution. It broadcasted "Joye Bangla" songs and the famous Choroipotro by M.R. Akhtar Mukul, which used biting satire to boost the morale of freedom fighters.
